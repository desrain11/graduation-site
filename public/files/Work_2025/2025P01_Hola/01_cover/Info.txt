Title: Hola the AI Healing Chatbot: User-Controlled Personalization to Enhance User Experience in Self-Disclosure

Time：2025.9-2026.1

Project Type: Graduation Project (Design & Design Research)

Role: UX Researcher

Location: Eindhoven

Client: Nexo Intelligent Technology Limited

EA Involved：Technology & Realization/Creativity & Aesthetics/User & Society/Business & Entrepreneurship/Math, Data & Computing

Abstract: Hola is an AI-powered daily healing mate designed for users aged 18–35, aiming to provide a private, low-judgment space for emotional disclosure and reflection. Through ongoing interaction, Hola adapts to users’ personalities and preferences, remembers their personal narratives, and offers real-time feedback to support self-reflection. With long-term companionship, the product seeks to help users gradually develop a more stable and positive mindset when facing everyday challenges.
